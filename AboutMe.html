Hi, I'm Alex

I am a passionate software developer, graduating in Electrical Engineering this year, who enjoys learning through action. At any given moment, there are guaranteed to be a few ongoing projects that I am working on, picking up new skills along the way.
I first found my love for computers at the age of 5, playing point and click adventure games (shoutout to pajama sam and freddy fish) and my first attempt at creating software was a choose-your-adventure style powerpoint file that had hundreds of pages with (somewhat) functional minigames although they were manually operated that I made in second grade.
Since then, I have studied at the University Of Calgary where I re-discovered my love for software creation, and will be graduating in April this year. Now, when I want to program a game, I use a game engine with C# scripting instead of powerpoint slides. School has taught me a variety of programming languages such as C, C++, Java, Matlab, and VHDL, but I have gone beyond learning from school and now have professional experience with python, SQL, Flux. Even now, I am learning new software (and networking) skills as I build this webserver, running a Node.Js server, load distributed using Nginx, hosted on a debian OS on a Rasberry Pi 3, and using a dynamic IP tracker to bypass the need to buy a static one which is how you are able to reach this page now!
